About This Project

This web application connects to NASA's official API to bring you the Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD). Each day, NASA features a different image or photograph of our fascinating universe, along with a brief explanation written by a professional astronomer.

I developed this project to explore the Flask micro web framework, containerization with Docker, and working with third-party APIs.

Check back daily for new cosmic wonders and expand your understanding of our universe!
